Privacy notice

Last updated: 14 July 2026

This validation website is operated by Enrique Folte under the working name Product Security Desk. Questions about this notice or personal data can be sent to hello@productsecuritydesk.com.

What this website collects

The website does not contain a contact form, advertising tracker, or non-essential cookie. It is hosted through GitHub Pages, a service of GitHub, Inc. GitHub may process limited technical logs, such as IP address, browser information, requested page, and timestamp, to deliver and protect the website.

When you contact us

If you send an email, we process the contact information and message you provide to answer the inquiry, assess engagement fit, maintain relevant business correspondence, and protect against abuse. Do not send source code, credentials, non-public vulnerabilities, incident evidence, or other sensitive product information through the initial contact email.

When you book a call

Booking links open Calendly, an external scheduling service. Calendly and the connected Google Calendar process the name, email address, time zone, meeting details, and any answers you provide to schedule and administer the call. Their own privacy terms apply on their services. Do not include source code, credentials, non-public vulnerabilities, incident evidence, or other sensitive product information in booking fields.

Legal basis and retention

Contact information is processed where necessary to respond to your request, take steps toward a possible contract, or pursue the legitimate interest of relevant business development. Inquiry records are retained only as long as reasonably needed for those purposes, legal obligations, or dispute handling.

Sharing and international processing

Information may be processed by GitHub for website hosting, Namecheap and Google for business email and calendar services, Calendly for scheduling, and relevant providers used for professional advice. Before accepting customer-confidential or highly sensitive material, we agree the relevant confidentiality, storage, access, subprocessor, retention, and deletion terms separately.

Your choices

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection regarding personal information. You may also object to direct marketing at any time by replying to the message or contacting the address above.

Changes

This notice will be updated when the website or data-handling process changes.
